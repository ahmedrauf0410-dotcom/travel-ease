About Travel Twin

We believe the best trips are shared — even when you start out solo.

OUR STORY

Built by Solo Travelers, for Solo Travelers

Travel Twin began in 2022 when two strangers met at a hostel and ended up traveling three countries together. We built the platform we wished existed — one that turns solo trips into shared ones, safely and intentionally.

Our Mission

Make solo travel safer, more affordable, and more connected by matching like-minded travelers around the world.

Our Vision

A world where no one has to choose between traveling solo and traveling alone.
