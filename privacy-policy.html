Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email, travel preferences, and payment details, as well as usage data to improve matching and recommendations.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information powers Travel Twin matching, booking, and the AI assistant's recommendations. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Profile Visibility

Your travel buddy and travel twin profile is visible to other verified members. You control which details appear publicly from your Profile settings.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption for payment and identity data, and restrict internal access on a need-to-know basis.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting our support team.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences and keep you signed in. You can disable cookies in your browser, though some features may not work correctly.

7. Contact Us

For privacy questions, reach us at our contact page or privacy@traveltwin.com.
